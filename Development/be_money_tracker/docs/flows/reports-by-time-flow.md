# reports by time flow

## Muc tieu

Phan tich xu huong chi tieu theo thoi gian de nguoi dung nhin thay muc tang/giam chi tieu va cac giai doan chi tieu bat thuong.

## Dieu kien truoc

- Nguoi dung da dang nhap.
- He thong co du giao dich trong khoang thoi gian duoc chon.

## Luong chinh

1. Nguoi dung chon khoang thoi gian can xem bao cao.
2. Nguoi dung chon cach nhom du lieu theo ngay, tuan hoac thang.
3. He thong tong hop tong thu va tong chi theo tung khoang thoi gian.
4. He thong so sanh cac khoang thoi gian de xac dinh xu huong tang, giam hoac on dinh.
5. He thong xac dinh khoang thoi gian co muc chi tieu cao nhat.
6. He thong tra ve ket qua de nguoi dung xem bieu do va nhan ra xu huong chi tieu.

## Luong thay the

- A1: Khong co du lieu trong khoang thoi gian $
ightarrow$ tra ve ket qua rong hoac gia tri 0.
- A2: Khoang thoi gian khong hop le $
ightarrow$ thong bao nguoi dung chon lai.
- A3: Nguoi dung khong co quyen truy cap du lieu $
ightarrow$ tu choi hien thi bao cao.

## Dau ra can co

- Danh sach tong thu, tong chi theo tung khoang thoi gian.
- Trang thai xu huong: tang, giam hoac on dinh.
- Khoang thoi gian chi tieu cao nhat.
